About Credential Verification

How it works: AmeenCheck uses blockchain-inspired digital signatures to ensure credential authenticity. Each credential has a unique ID and cryptographic signature that cannot be forged.

What you can verify: Background check certificates, employment verifications, education verifications, and other credentials issued through the AmeenCheck platform.

Security: All credentials are timestamped and digitally signed. The verification process is instant and does not require any login or personal information.
